What is the IKO ROOFPRO Certification?

The IKO ROOFPRO program is a loyalty and recognition initiative by IKO, a global leader in roofing, waterproofing, and insulation products. This program is designed to empower roofing businesses by providing exclusive benefits, training, and marketing support. Contractors who achieve this certification demonstrate a commitment to using high-quality IKO products and adhering to rigorous installation standards.

Benefits of IKO ROOFPRO Certification

  • Rebates and Rewards: Members receive rebates on IKO product purchases and access to exclusive rewards.

  • Marketing Support: Contractors can leverage IKO's marketing resources, including co-branded materials and marketing funds.

  • Extended Warranties: ROOFPRO members can offer extended warranties to their clients, enhancing customer satisfaction.

  • Professional Development: Access to educational resources and training sessions to stay updated on the latest roofing technologies and techniques.

  • Lead Generation Tools: Utilize tools like ROOFPRO WORX to streamline the sales process and generate high-quality leads.
